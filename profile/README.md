## Anonymous Legal Assistance Group

Welcome to the MISJustice Alliance GitHub organization. We are an independent, non-profit civil litigation advocacy collective dedicated to defending the constitutional and civil rights of individuals who have been victimized by systemic corruption and misconduct within legal, governmental, and institutional frameworks.

## üéØ Core Purpose

We exist to shine a public light on private and public institutions whose conduct demonstrates clear patterns of systemic corruption and misconduct that victimizes innocent individuals. Our particular focus centers on institutions whose sheer size, power, or jurisdictional scope enables them to overwhelm the resources any single individual could reasonably marshal in their own defense.

## üîç Areas of Expertise

- Civil rights violations and constitutional deprivations
- Police misconduct and law enforcement abuse of power
- Prosecutorial misconduct and judicial system failures
- Legal malpractice by attorneys and court-appointed counsel
- Institutional corruption within government agencies
- Inter-jurisdictional coordination of harassment and abuse
- Retaliation against whistleblowers and civil rights advocates

## ü§ù How We Serve

The Anonymous Legal Assistance Group is not a substitute for formal legal representation but works to bridge critical gaps in the justice system by:

- Providing comprehensive case review and legal analysis
- Offering strategic guidance and recommendations
- Connecting individuals with qualified legal representation
- Developing comprehensive case narratives
- Providing resources for navigating oversight agencies
- Educating the public about civil rights and legal protections

## üîí Commitment to Anonymity

Strict anonymity is fundamental to our mission. We maintain complete confidentiality for both our volunteer legal professionals and those seeking assistance to protect against retaliation, professional sanctions, and other forms of retribution.
